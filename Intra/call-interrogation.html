The calltypes found on the left-hand side bar of this page will correspond to call categories found in our Computer Aided Dispatch system.
You will find descriptions of these calltypes, as well as questioning prompts for our phone interviews.
It should be noted and understood that these are not scripts to read from, and the questions can be largely situational.
This resource exists to help you develop and strengthen your skills. Improvisation is one of these skills that can't be written down.
If at any time you feel an addition, subtraction, or change should be made to any of these calltypes, please write it down, and submit it to your trainer, or a supervisor.
