Public Intoxication calls can be time-critical to the safety of both the suspect, as well as the general public. We want to offer intervention in the form of Law Enforcement dispatch before an intoxicated subject gets into a vehicle, stumbles and falls into traffic, or passes out while exposed to elements. We also want to avoid potentially causing a disturbance or comitting a crime.

There is always a possibility that a subject having a medical emergency is misreported as public intoxication. Several medical conditions including diabetic problems can mirror heavy intoxication.
