ZETX is a free service provided to Emergency Services agencies and is used to obtain carrier and subscriber information for a given mobile number.
ZETX has limitations that should be expected. If a subscriber purchases a pre-paid phone plan you can obtain carrier information but no subscriber name.
This is a fantastic resource to use prior to beginning a ping to obtain GPS information as you will know exactly which carrier's compliance department to contact
Due to unforseen complications when registering for this service, you will have to use Jake Andrews as the login name, and communications@mcmo.us for the login email address.
